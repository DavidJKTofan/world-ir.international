World of International Relations

A nonprofit and politically independent association of young change-makers,
which was founded on the basic notion of networking and global leadership development.

  • Learn more

Networking

Building a diverse global network of knowledgeable individuals and learning from their experiences and stories.

  • Learn more

Leadership Development

Helping our members grow professionally by helping them support a wide range of projects, as well as providing a variety of resources and opportunities.

  • Learn more

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Supporting the Global Goals through a range of initiatives, with focus on Goal 4 (Quality Education), Goal 5 (Gender Equality), as well as Goal 17 (Partnerships for the Goals).

Who we are

We love to be part of amazing projects and networks.
Below a brief overview of projects and networks of which we are a part of or which we support:

SDSN Youth

An initiative launched by UN Secretary General, Ban Ki-moon, in 2012 to mobilize global expertise around the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Youth Employment Decade

An international movement of reflection, thought, debate and action, that provides ideas, content and solutions for the unemployment situation or the situation of no decent employment that millions of youths are experiencing in hundreds of places across the world.

UNSA Spain

UNSA Spain is a spanish national federation of associations with the purpose to bring the United Nations closer to the young spanish society.

Spanish Youth Delegation to the United Nations

This programme – the UN Youth Delegate Programme – offers young people the opportunity to defend their rights and interests at the national and international level, actively participating in global and intergovernmental processes.

The Bridge Project

A programme to promote entrepreneurship, with focus on entrepreneurship in the Dominican Republic.
